Opinion Mining Engine
How detection works
1
NLP Preprocessing — tokenizes, lowercases and cleans the review text
2
Sentiment Analysis — TextBlob-style polarity scoring using pos/neg word lexicons
3
TF-IDF Feature Extraction — key phrase detection and spam phrase matching
4
Logistic Regression Classifier — predicts Fake / Genuine with confidence score
5
Mismatch Detection — flags sentiment-star rating anomalies
Detection signals
• Positive text + 1–2 star rating → suspicious
• Negative text + 4–5 star rating → suspicious
• Extremely short text with extreme rating
• Repeated filler / generic phrases
• Promotional / ad language
• Excessive special characters or emoji overuse
